Privacy

Short version: this tool reads only your own public shop/closet pages, in your own browser, and sends none of it to us.

What it does

The bookmarklet and the browser extension run entirely in your own browser tab while you are on your own Depop shop page or Vinted closet page. They read the listing information that is already publicly visible on those pages (title, description, price, brand, size, condition, and photo URLs) and format it into copy/paste-ready drafts.

What we collect

Nothing. Your listing data is processed and displayed entirely in your browser. It is never sent to, stored on, or logged by this website's server. There are no accounts, and we never ask for or handle any Depop or Vinted login, password, or cookie.

Permissions the extension asks for

  • Access to depop.com and vinted.com — so the button can read the listings on the shop/closet page you are viewing. It does nothing on any other site.
  • Scripting — to run the read-and-format script on that page when you click the button.

It has no access to your browsing history, other tabs, or any other website.

Third parties

No analytics, no trackers, no advertising, no data sharing. The site is hosted on Render, which sees standard web-server request logs (IP address, timestamp) as any website's host does; those logs contain none of your listing data.

Contact

Questions? Reach out via the GitHub repository linked from the tool.
