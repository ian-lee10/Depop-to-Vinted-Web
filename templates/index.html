Depop Vinted
  1. Drag this to your bookmarks bar: ↳ Depop to Vinted
  2. Go to your Depop shop page (depop.com/yourusername) — the page showing your listing grid.
  3. Click the bookmark. This tab will then show your Vinted-ready drafts.
Vinted Depop
  1. Drag this to your bookmarks bar: ↳ Vinted to Depop
  2. Go to your Vinted closet page (your profile — the page showing your listing grid).
  3. Click the bookmark. This tab will then show your Depop-ready drafts.

No login, no cookie-copying, either way — this all runs in your own browser tab. Don't see a bookmarks bar? Cmd+Shift+B on Mac, Ctrl+Shift+B on Windows/Linux shows it.
